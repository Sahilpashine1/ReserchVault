## 🔧 What Happens After Deployment

### Your Team Will Get:
- **Frontend URL**: `https://your-app.vercel.app`
  - Share this with your team
  - They can access from anywhere
  - No installation needed!

- **Backend URL**: `https://your-api.onrender.com`
  - Keep this private
  - Used by frontend automatically

### How It Works:
1. Team members visit your Vercel URL
2. They register with their email
3. They login and use the system
4. All data is stored in cloud database
5. Everyone can access from anywhere!
